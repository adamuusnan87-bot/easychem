Quick Reference

Common Concentrations

  • Physiological saline: 0.9% NaCl (~0.15 M)
  • Vinegar: ~0.8 M acetic acid
  • Battery acid: ~4 M H₂SO₄
  • Household bleach: ~0.8 M NaOCl

Common Volumes

  • Test tube: 10-20 mL
  • Beaker: 50-1000 mL
  • Volumetric flask: 25, 50, 100, 250, 500, 1000 mL
  • Graduated cylinder: 10-1000 mL

Safety Tips

  • Always add acid to water, not water to acid
  • Wear appropriate PPE
  • Work in a well-ventilated area
  • Label all solutions clearly
